Project Description and Results.

BRIDGE INC.

Bridge is a mobile application that helps an individual find volunteer opportunities with a simple app. Organizations post volunteer opportunites on a daily basis and bridge displays them for the public to see. Bridge creates a figurative connection between an individual and the community.

THE PROPOSAL

Bridge needed a UI that was like a newsfeed where users to scroll through each day and view daily opportunities. The newsfeed would also allow individuals to sign up to volunteer at events.
