Project Description and Results.

SUMMIT INC.

Summit was a start-up that my team and I started in 2014. Students, such as ourselves, have to go through the hassle of taking notes each day. We thought of creating a mobile application that took notes for you by simply snapping a picture of any printed text. This automatic note-taking provides students with an opportunity to focus more on the maeterial and not worry about the notes that are required to be submitted.

THE PROPOSAL

As talented mobile app developers, my team and I knew we needed to make the app work efficiently, quickly, and easily. To do this, we needed to consider a very simple user interface that could handle the complexities of the back-end code. We also wanted the website to remain simple and easy-to-use.

THE RESULTS

The user-interface I made allowed users to take notes with two simple taps. The user taps the camera button, which launches the stock camera and simply taps again to capture an image. After this process, the magic of Summit takes over as it uses algorithms and APIs to take notes for you. The website also embodies this simplicity that allows users to instantly subscribe for the beta list.
