Project Description and Results.

TAUX INC.

Taux was a start-up that my team and I started in 2014. The challenge of sharing music has been one that our society has seen throughout decades. Sharing YouTube links, poor quality voice recordings, and even singing is never like the real thing. Taux was made to share clips of high quality music over chats with your friends and family.

THE PROPOSAL

As talented mobile app developers, my team and I knew we needed to make the app work efficiently, quickly, and easily. To do this, we needed to consider a very simple user interface that could handle the complexities of the back-end code. We also wanted the website to remain simple and easy-to-use.

THE RESULTS

Both the website and the user-interface that I designed and built embraced the company colors to maintain consistency. The mobile UI was meant to allow users to share music seamlessly without many taps. We kept the tap count low and the satisfaction very high.
