Project Description and Results.

Calc4u

Calc4u was a calculus project that my partner and I created for our classmates to help them study for the class final. The app was a huge success and helped many students earn high grades on the final.

THE PROPOSAL

The app needed to be simple because in order to cram, students needed to focus more on the material we provided then the rest of the app.

THE RESULTS

The app was very easy to use and the user-interface was "stunning" (as quoted by most students that used the app). We did not get a chance to make a website for the app and quite frankly did not need one because we have no intention of monetizing the app.
